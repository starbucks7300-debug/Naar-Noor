## Executive Overview

The **Naar-Noor** restaurant management platform has been fully delivered as a production-ready application. Customers can browse menus, book tables, write reviews, and explore chef profiles—all available in English and Arabic. Restaurant staff can manage menus, view reservations, handle customer inquiries, and monitor operations. The entire platform is secured, tested, and ready for immediate deployment.
